Conservation Management Plan for Biological Corridor 03, covering the 2023-2033 planning period for the corridor connecting Jigme Singye Wangchuck National Park, Phibsoo Wildlife Sanctuary, and Royal Manas National Park. The plan frames ecological connectivity and species persistence as the core management objective for this landscape connector.
