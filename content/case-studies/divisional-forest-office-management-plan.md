Prepared the Divisional Forest Office Management Plan as an applied planning framework for coordinating forest governance, conservation priorities, resource management, and field operations at the divisional level.

The plan translated ecological, administrative, and community-linked management needs into structured actions for day-to-day implementation, helping guide operational decision-making across protected, production, and community forest landscapes.
